HOW TO PLAY

Guess the Birdle in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid 7 letter word, the answer will be the name of a bird that lives in the UK. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the bird.

Examples
